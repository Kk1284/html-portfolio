The Best Movies According to Kritik

My Top 3 movies of all time.

Ye Jawani Hai Deewani

On a trekking trip, an introvert falls for a charming ex-classmate, whose thirst for adventure drives them apart.Years later their paths cross again

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

Three friends on an adventorous road trip/bachelor party in Spain learn some lessons about each other that will change them forever.

Dear Zindagi

A young cinematographer with a bright professional future and a lack of empathy learns the importance of balancing ambition with humanity
